## Initial Notes

In ulp_distance_with_special: +0.0 and -0.0 are 1 ULP apart in IEEE ordered representation (adjacent representable values). A 1-ULP tolerance ACCEPTS the wrong sign for zero. This is documented as the B1 gap rationale for bit_exact_checks. Worth a unit test that explicitly verifies this: ulp_distance(+0.0, -0.0) == 1, not 0.
