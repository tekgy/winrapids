Created: 2026-04-12T15:23:10-05:00

## Initial Notes

pred type is bool — it cannot carry NaN. fcmp_gt(NaN,x)=false (IEEE). This means pred-producing ops can't signal NaN; they silently produce false. I11 enforcement requires callers to guard before comparison. Is there a case for a three-valued pred (true/false/nan)? Probably not, but worth naming.
