## Initial Notes

TC-3 concern: sampling strategy for libm domain coverage. Uniform in [0, MAX_DOUBLE] is biased toward large exponents. Better: log-uniform (uniform in exponent field). For tam_exp specifically: near-zero inputs (where exp(x) ≈ 1+x, polynomial precision test) are undersampled if using linear-uniform. Math-researcher should document their sampling strategy explicitly.
