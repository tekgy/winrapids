## Initial Notes

Every polynomial in Phase 1 libm uses Remez-optimized coefficients. The adversarial role has not yet audited HOW these coefficients are generated. gen-constants.py and remez.py exist in peak2-libm/ but their outputs haven't been independently verified. For 1-ULP accuracy the polynomial error must be < 0.5 ULP on the fit domain AND the coefficient rounding must be accounted for. Audit: (1) does remez.py verify the approximation error after coefficient rounding? (2) Are coefficients stored at sufficient precision in libm-constants.toml?
