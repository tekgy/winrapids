## Initial Notes

The Cody-Waite constants LN2_HI and LN2_LO in tam_exp.toml are asserted as correct (LN2_HI = f64::from_bits(0x3FE62E42FEFA3800)). The adversarial has not independently verified: (1) that LN2_HI has enough trailing zeros for n*LN2_HI to be exact (Sterbenz), (2) that LN2_LO correctly captures the residual ln(2) - LN2_HI to full fp64 precision, (3) that the two-constant sum LN2_HI + LN2_LO rounds to ln(2) to > 53 bits. Verify against mpmath at 200+ digits before campsite 2.6 opens.
