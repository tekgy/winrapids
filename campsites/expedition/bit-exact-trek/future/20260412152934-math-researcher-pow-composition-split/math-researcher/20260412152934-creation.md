## Initial Notes

pow is structurally two functions welded together: real-valued power for a>0 via exp(b*log(a)), and integer power for a<0 via repeated squaring. Phase 2 design: split into tam_pow_real and tam_pow_int explicitly, with tam_pow being a front-end dispatch. Cleaner than the current integer-power-as-fast-path framing
