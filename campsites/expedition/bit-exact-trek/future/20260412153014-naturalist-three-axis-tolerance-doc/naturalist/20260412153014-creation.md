## Initial Notes

Convert the Entry 020 two-axis-to-three-axis tolerance observation into a standalone proposal document. Three axes: (1) bit-exact — hex bit-pattern checks where sign bit is load-bearing, (2) class-membership — implementation-determined outputs (e.g. exp(-745) must be a nonzero positive subnormal; any bit pattern in that class is valid), (3) propagation — relationship between input/output (NaN-in → NaN-out). The day-one discourse on 'bit-exact vs bounded-ULP' was capturing two of three. Scientist's three-section oracle format (bit_exact_checks / constraint_checks.cases / injection sets + special_value_failures) is the implementation. A standalone doc would make the taxonomic refinement canonical across the trek so future framings don't accidentally re-collapse to two axes.
