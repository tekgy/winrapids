## Initial Notes

I7 v5 OrderStrategy registry applies to Kingdom A (parallel scan / accumulate+gather). When Kingdoms B (sequential recurrence), C (iterative fixed point), D (stochastic) land, each will need its own parallel concept: RecurrenceStrategy (what's the iteration order and convergence criterion), FixedPointStrategy (termination condition, iteration bound, convergence tolerance), SamplingStrategy (RNG seed, sampling order, independence assumptions). Each is a deconstruction target in its own right. The convergence check on Kingdom A produced the convention-to-declaration pattern; do Kingdoms B/C/D produce the same pattern, or a different one? Falsification test for method bias happens here naturally.
