## Initial Notes

Adversarial's P17 bug was Sign(NaN) returning 0 instead of NaN. 'Sign' is a sign-sensitive op: its output depends on IEEE-754 sign bit (±0 distinguishable, NaN has undefined sign but should propagate). How many OTHER sign-sensitive ops exist in the tambear primitive catalog? Candidates: sign(x), copysign(x, y), signbit(x), ilogb(x), classify(x), isnegative(x), ispositive(x), the sign component of decomposition ops like frexp. Each needs explicit sign-handling policy per I11-equivalent rule. Worth a systematic survey: for every primitive that touches sign, what's the declared behavior on ±0, NaN, ±Inf, subnormal? The survey either (a) confirms the existing primitives are clean, or (b) surfaces more I17-like invariant gaps. Pair well with the exception-semantics-surface-area campsite.
