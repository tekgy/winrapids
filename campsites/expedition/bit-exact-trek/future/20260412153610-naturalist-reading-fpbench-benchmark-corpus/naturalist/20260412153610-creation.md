## Initial Notes

Reading/tool: FPBench (http://fpbench.org, github.com/FPBench/FPBench) is a benchmark suite of fp-sensitive programs used by the formal-methods community to compare numerical-accuracy tools. It has a standard input format (FPCore) and a collection of ~100 programs across domains (signal processing, scientific computing, finance, graphics) that are known to trigger precision issues. Adversarial's hard-cases suite could potentially consume FPBench inputs as additional test cases. The corpus is much larger than anything the team can generate manually. Worth investigation by adversarial: can FPBench programs be converted to .tam IR (or their inputs/outputs used as reference cases)? Does FPBench have specific tests for the Bucket B corners we care about? This would extend the adversarial reach significantly with minimal work.
