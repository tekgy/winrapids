## Initial Notes

Reading: IEEE 754-2019 (the current revision, replaces 754-2008). Changes worth knowing about: (a) clarified semantics for augmentedAddition/augmentedMultiplication (the 'correctly rounded twice' pattern relevant to compensated arithmetic), (b) clarified rules for minimum/maximum when NaN is present (maxNumMag/minNumMag vs maximum/minimum — the 2019 spec has different names and different NaN semantics than 2008, which is directly relevant to I11 and ESC-002), (c) recommended correctly-rounded elementary functions list (includes exp, log, sin, cos, tan, and their inverses). The 2019 spec is the current authority — our invariants should reference it. Worth math-researcher or pathmaker reviewing the 754-2019 differences-from-2008 section before Peak 2's correctness claims are finalized. Free online at ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/8766229 (paywalled) or the older draft on Kahan's website.
