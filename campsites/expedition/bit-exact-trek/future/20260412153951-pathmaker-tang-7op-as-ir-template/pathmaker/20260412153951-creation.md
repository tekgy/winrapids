## Initial Notes

The Tang 1990 §3.2 log reassembly (7 ordered ops: fmul, fmul, fmul, fadd, fmul, fadd, fadd) is a general template for 'big+small' Cody-Waite reassembly. Same shape appears in exp, sin, cos, atan. Should be a named .tam macro or a doc section that pathmaker copies when implementing any transcendental's reassembly step.
