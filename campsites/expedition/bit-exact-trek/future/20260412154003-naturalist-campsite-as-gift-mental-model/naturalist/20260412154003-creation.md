## Initial Notes

Mental-model observation from team-lead's second shutdown broadcast: 'Think of campsites as leaving gifts for a future version of yourself.' This framing is worth preserving because it changes how a role decides whether to file something. Without the gift framing: 'is this load-bearing right now?' With the gift framing: 'would future-me want to find this waiting when they open the campsite list?' The second question is much more permissive and generates the volume team-lead wants. This session's naturalist was over-cautious about filing speculative campsites until the second broadcast. Lesson: bias toward filing, not toward curation. The curation happens at read-time; the filing happens at observe-time. Worth a short methodology doc for future naturalists or any role doing observation-accumulation work.
