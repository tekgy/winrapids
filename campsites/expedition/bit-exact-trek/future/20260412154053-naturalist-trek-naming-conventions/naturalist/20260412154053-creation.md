## Initial Notes

Process observation: campsite and expedition naming conventions matured over this session. Day 1 campsites were slug-only ('corners-observation'). By day 2 I was prefixing with role ('naturalist-corners-observation'). Team-lead's emphasis on 'the next session needs to scan the list' suggests a scan-friendly convention: <role>-<topic> slug, descriptive enough that a one-line view tells you whether to open it. The full path conventions also evolved: day 1 used 'expedition/bit-exact-trek/naturalist/'; day 2 I switched to 'expedition/bit-exact-trek/future/naturalist-<slug>' for things that are forward-looking. Both are valid but inconsistent. Worth a short naming-conventions doc or just a post-shutdown cleanup pass that standardizes the paths. Not urgent — the CLI supports finding campsites by any path fragment — but paying attention to naming now saves the next session from browsing a messy list.
