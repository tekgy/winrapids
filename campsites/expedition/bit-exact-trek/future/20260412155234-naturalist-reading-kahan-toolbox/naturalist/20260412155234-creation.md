## Initial Notes

Reading: Kahan's personal website at people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~wkahan has many unpublished notes on fp arithmetic pitfalls, paranoia tests, double-rounding, and the Table Maker's Dilemma. Several of these predate their eventual publication and contain insights you can't find anywhere else. Kahan is the father of IEEE 754 and his unpublished notes are closer to oral tradition than formal literature. Worth a browse by math-researcher or aristotle — especially 'A Logarithm Too Clever By Half' and the various Paranoia-test documents. URL: people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~wkahan/
