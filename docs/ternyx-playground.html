E Columns — Emission Signals

E columns are just more compute output. The leaf evaluates thresholds on its DOs and outputs F32 flags. 14 leaves produce 68 emission signals.

Two Paths — Structural Separation

LIVE PATH (ephemeral)
Compute → E in memory → detect → act → real trade
If detect daemon wasn't running, signal is gone. Can't retroactively fire.
RESEARCH PATH (persistent)
E columns on disk → research daemon → shadow trades
Zero foreknowledge. Hundreds of strategies simultaneously. Builds track records.
